FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Saas Demo. Can't find an answer? Contact us anytime.

How secure is Saas Demo?

Security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption both in transit and at rest, regular security audits, and compliance with industry standards including SOC 2, GDPR, and HIPAA. All data is stored securely with strict access controls.

Can I use Saas Demo on mobile?

Absolutely! Saas Demo offers fully-featured iOS and Android apps that allow you to stay connected with your team wherever you are. All core functionality including messaging, file sharing, and video calls are available on mobile.

How does the free plan work?

Our free plan is designed for small teams and individuals. You get access to all core messaging features, 1:1 video calls, and up to 5GB of storage for your team. There are no time limits - you can use the free plan indefinitely.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll only pay for what you use, and we'll prorate any differences in cost.
